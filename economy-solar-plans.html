Frequently Asked Questions

What is the warranty on this plan?

All components come with a manufacturer's warranty. The inverter and panels typically have a 10-25 year warranty, while the battery has a 5-year warranty. We provide a 1-year warranty on the installation workmanship.

How long does installation take?

A standard installation for the Economy plan is typically completed within 1-2 business days, depending on your property's specifics and weather conditions.

What maintenance is required?

Solar systems are very low maintenance. We recommend cleaning the panels once or twice a year to ensure optimal performance. We also offer affordable maintenance packages.
