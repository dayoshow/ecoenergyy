Frequently Asked Questions

What is the warranty on the Advanced Plan? expand_more

The Advanced Plan comes with a comprehensive warranty covering all components. The inverter has a 5-year warranty, the batteries have a 3-year warranty, and the solar panels have a 25-year performance warranty. Please refer to the detailed warranty document for specific terms and conditions.

How long does installation take? expand_more

Typical installation for the Advanced Plan takes 2-3 days, depending on site specifics and weather conditions. Our team works efficiently to minimize disruption.

What maintenance is required? expand_more

Solar systems require minimal maintenance. We recommend periodic cleaning of the solar panels and an annual check-up to ensure optimal performance, which our team can provide.
